Movie Review

Elf Rating: 7/10 Genre: Comedy Release Year: 2003

On Christmas Eve, an orphaned baby crawls into Santa Claus's sack at an orphanage and is accidentally taken back to the North Pole. The elves discover the infant and name him Buddy, inspired by the label on his diaper. Papa Elf adopts Buddy and raises him as one of his own.

Reviewed By: John Smith Rating: 8/10
The film boasts a terrific cast, especially Will Ferrell, who pulls out probably one of his best performances to date as Buddy the Elf. Lots of funny and hilarious moments, mostly utilising Buddy's naivety and innocence. It has a near-seamless mix between live-action and Rankin/Bass-esque stop motion.
